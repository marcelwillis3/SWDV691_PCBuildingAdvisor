How To Use

  1. Start from the home page.
  2. Select your motherboard from the Mobos dropdown.
  3. Select your central processing unit from the CPUs dropdown.
  4. Select your graphical processing unit from the GPUs dropdown.
  5. Select your power supply from the PSUs dropdown.
  6. Click the analyze button.
  7. View your analysis results in the window below.
